About Me

I was born in Stillwater, Oklahoma. I moved a total of 13 times before finally settling down in Seattle. I grew up playing baseball, skateboarding, and playing guitar. All of which I still like to do to this day. I went to Roosevelt High School and then the University of Colorado Boulder, where I will graduate from in May 2021. I try to run, lift weights, throw the football, and go for a ride on my skateboard everyday. I find if I do those things as breaks from my work, I tend to be more productive throughout the day. I am quite busy nowadays, as I am taking five classes at CU and a coding bootcamp with DU. However, I am keeping up fine and staying happy despite everything going on in the world.
